Rocket Launcher Turret
    Tier 3
    
    This is the fourth turret you gain access to. Like the Grenade Launcher Turret,
    this turret hurls explosives at your enemies, except this time it chases and explodes
    on them with pinpoint accuracy.
    
    Range (in blocks): 30
    Accuracy: Pin Point
    Ammo type: Homing Roket
    Minimum Tier: 3 (Hardened)

    Projectile Damage: 5 Hearts
    AOE radius: 5
    Shots/s: 0.7
    RF per shot: 5000RF
