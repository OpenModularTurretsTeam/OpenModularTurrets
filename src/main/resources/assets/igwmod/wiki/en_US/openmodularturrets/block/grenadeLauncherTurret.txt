Grenade Launcher Turret
    Tier 2
    
    This is the third turret you gain access to.
    It is capable of lobbing tiny balls of of death (grenades) decent distances
    and do damage to everything around it, when it explodes.
    
    Range (in blocks): 20
    Accuracy: Medium
    Ammo type: Grenade
    Minimum Tier: 2 (reinforced)

    Projectile Damage: 4 Hearts
    AOE radius: 3
    Shots/s: 0.5
    RF per shot: 3000RF
